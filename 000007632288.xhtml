Auto Blocker provides an extra level of protection for your apps and data by preventing installation of apps from unauthorized sources, and blocking malicious commands from being sent to your device using a USB cable. Auto Blocker also prevents installation of system software using a USB cable, and blocks malicious images in messaging apps. Auto Blocker is available on Galaxy devices running One UI 6 or higher. To get the most protection, make sure Auto Blocker is updated to the latest version and is turned on in Settings > Security and privacy.From One UI 6.1.1, Auto Blocker provides extra-strong protection through the “Maximum restrictions” feature as an option for people who need more security. While enabling “Maximum restrictions,” some of your device’s features will be restricted. “Maximum restrictions” turns on the “App protection” feature, and prevents activation of device admin apps and work profiles. “Maximum restrictions” also blocks some features of Samsung Messages and Gallery.
