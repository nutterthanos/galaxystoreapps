ChargedUp is there when you need it; rent a power bank and you can take it with you to return at over 2000 stations across Europe! What is ChargedUp? ChargedUp has created a station network for portable power bank. Rent a power bank and use it while at that venue, or take it with you and charge on the go. Power banks come with their own lightning port, no need to have your own cable. How does it work? Open the ChargedUp app to find your closest station Scan the QR code on the station to rent a power bank Plug in using the cable included and charge on the go Return the power bank to any station shown in the app’s map Where can I find ChargedUp? We’ve partnered with popular bars, cafes, shops and more. We’re always looking for new and exciting venues to partner with. Drop us a message in the app and tell us where you’d like to see us next. How do I pay? Set up your form of payment in the app, including cards and Google Pay. Scan a station’s QR code, select your pricing plan and rent a power bank it’ll pop out of a station like a piece of toast! No stress; we cap your daily spending and prompt you to remember to return it. If you choose to keep it, you'll be charged its purchase price and then be able to swap it across our network.
