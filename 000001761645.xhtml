Samsung Pay transmits a one-time use digital card number each time a transaction is made. Samsung Pay is protected by Samsung KNOX® while transactions can only be authorized with your fingerprint or PIN. For added security, you can remotely lock or erase your payment cards in Samsung Pay using the ‘Samsung Find My Mobile’ service.
