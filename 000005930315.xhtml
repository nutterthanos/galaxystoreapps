In addition to operators "traditional" or post-production influence over preinstalled applications; Ignite enables MNOs to install or delete apps with or without the user’s permission. A unique attribute that extends the MNOs influence over pre-installed software. The technology enables the installation of applications on a new device without the need of a ROM version change. Ignite is preinstalled on the ROM and begins to operate from the devices first activation (first boot). At first boot, the user will receive a list of applications to install from a remote server (according to relevant parameters). They will then proceed to install the APK files and create shortcuts on the home screen. Once the process is complete, push notification campaigns can be put into effect as powerful marketing tools in order to promote specific apps that correlate to the user's interests. All installations, whether they are running in the background or approved by the user, are transparent and can be removed/uninstalled by the user. Ignite’s software knows how to identify the type of device in use while monitoring and controlling the number of apps downloaded. The servers also keep statistical records of how many apps have been successfully or unsuccessfully installed. This information is instrumental as it helps MNOs and marketers to understand the user’s interests and enables performance based campaigns (CPI models).
